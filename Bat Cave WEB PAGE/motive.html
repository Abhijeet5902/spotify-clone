Our Motive

"In this community, let's unite like the Dark Knight and inspire each other to be the heroes our neighborhood needs."

This motive highlights the idea of coming together as a community, just like Batman does with Gotham City, to make a positive impact and support one another.

It would be great to see more fanart and creative content being shared in the Batman community. It's always exciting to see different interpretations of the character and the Gotham universe.
